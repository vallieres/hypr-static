Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on Hypr Social's website.

Information we collect

New Hypr Social accounts are created with an email address and password.

Email addresses are only used for logging in, password resets, responding to emails that you initiate, sending notifications that you request, and information on new features released to the site. We don't send promotional emails.

We store information about your social media posts, information you add to your Hypr profile, as well as statistics on interaction with your Hypr Page and posts.

We also collect anonymous statistics from visitors to help in identifying popular section of the website and help us plan new features.

Technical basics

If you link your account to one of your social media account, we must store one or multiple tokens to pull in your great content.

If you upload files to Hypr Social, we need to store them until you delete them.

We do not store any social network username or password on your behalf, nor do we ask you to provide them.

We use cookies on the site to keep you logged in.

Our server software may also store basic technical information, such as your IP address, in temporary memory or logs.

Hypr Social uses the following hosting and service providers, which also have access to basic technical information passing through their services and data stored using their resources:

  • OpalStack (Privacy policy)
  • Bunny (Privacy policy)

Analytics

Hypr Social's website collects aggregate, anonymous statistics, such as the percentage of users who use particular features, to improve the service.

Information usage

We use the information we collect to operate and improve our website, and customer support.

We do not share personal information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to accomplish Hypr Social's functionality.

We may disclose your information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal requirements; to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud or abuse, violations of our policies; or to protect our rights and property.

In the future, we may sell to, buy, merge with, or partner with other businesses. In such transactions, user information may be among the transferred assets.

Security

We implement a variety of security measures to help keep your information secure. For instance, all communication with the app and website requires HTTPS Passwords are hashed, not stored, using industry-standard methods (currently Argon2). And we always ask for the minimum possible permission when you authorize Hypr Social to access your social network accounts.

Accessing, changing, or deleting information

You may access or change your information or delete your account from the Hypr Social dashboard.

Deleted information may be kept in backups for up to 90 days. Backups are encrypted and are only accessed if needed for disaster recovery.

Hypr Social may delete your information at any time and for any reason, such as technical needs, legal concerns, abuse prevention, removal of idle accounts, data loss, or any other reason.

Third-party links and content

Hypr Social displays links and content from third-party social network and sites, and downloads social post media directly from each social network's third-party servers. These have their own independent privacy policies, and we have no responsibility or liability for their content or activities.

California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We comply with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.

Information for European Union Customers

By using Hypr Social and providing your information, you authorize us to collect, use, and store your information outside of the European Union.

International Transfers of Information

Information may be processed, stored, and used outside of the country in which you are located. Data privacy laws vary across jurisdictions, and different laws may be applicable to your data depending on where it is processed, stored, or used.

Your Consent

By using our site or apps, you consent to our privacy policy.

Contacting Us

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, you may email privacy@Hypr Social.fm. Please note that account deletion should be done within the Hypr Social website, not via email requests, for security reasons.
