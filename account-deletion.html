Account Deletion

Looking for information on how to permanently delete your account and remove all information we might have on your person? You found the right page!

Photo by Sam Pak

Instructions

We do not take care of deletion requests by email, we have the feature built in our service where you can take care of your account deletion yourself!

Login to Authenticate

Log in to your account as you normally do it with your usual email and password.

This is so that we know you are the account owner, and the sole individual that is authorized to do any changes to your account.

Navigate to Delete Account

From the left menu, navigate to the Delete Account section (or use this link to reach that page).

Confirmation

Please follow the Instructions to confirm that you are the account owner, and confirm that you understand that this is permanent and that we cannot undo the deletion, even if you contact us.

Confirmation

Once you click the big red button, your account and all your personal information will be removed from our servers, permanently.
